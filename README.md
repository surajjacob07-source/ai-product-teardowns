# AI Product Teardowns

A public repo of structured teardowns of AI products, features, and workflows.

## Why this repo exists
This repo documents product thinking around AI experiences: what problem a feature solves, who it serves, where friction exists, how success should be measured, and what a smarter roadmap could look like.

## What each teardown includes
- Product / feature overview
- Target user and job-to-be-done
- User journey
- What works well
- Friction and gaps
- AI-specific product risks
- KPI / metrics tree
- Prioritized improvements
- PM recommendation

## Structure
- `teardowns/` — individual analyses
- `templates/` — reusable teardown templates

## Starter teardowns
- ChatGPT Projects
- Perplexity for research workflows

## Signal this repo is meant to send
- Strong AI product intuition
- Clear PM communication
- User-centered critique
- Decision-making under tradeoffs
